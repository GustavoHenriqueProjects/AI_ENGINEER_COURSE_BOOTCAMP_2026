TYPE=TECHNOLOGY | SECTION=CLINICAL_APPLICATION | THEME=PRODUCT | COUNTRY=GLOBAL
Brain4care technology performs non-invasive monitoring of intracranial compliance. It provides data that support neurological diagnosis, enable predictive medical decisions, increase patient safety, and improve clinical outcomes. It is used by neurologists and neurosurgeons.
